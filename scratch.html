Why I Bake

Baking has always been one of my favorite hobbies. I have fond memories of baking with my mom in the kitchen. Growing up, I was obsesseed with cooking shows and I would bake my own birthday cake every year. I love trying new recipes and sharing them with friends and family.

My Favorite Things to Bake

A few of my favorite things to bake:

  1. Cookies
    • Chocolate chip- a calssic, warm goeey choclate chip cookie always hits the spot.
    • Snickerdoodles- my dad's favorite cookie
    • Pumpkin Spice Cookies- my favorite to make in the Fall.
  2. Bread
    • Banana bread- a classic sweet treat, snack, and breakfast food.
    • Sour Dough- need I say more...
  3. Pies
    • Choclate Cream Pie- My grandmas recipe never fails to please the crowds
    • Key Lime Pie- Being from Florida, Key Lime Pie is a classic. Publix has the best!
    • Apple Pie- A warm slice of apple pie with a scoop of ice cream could cure all my worries.
